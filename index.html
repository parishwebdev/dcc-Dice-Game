CHŌ-HAN (AKA CHŌ-HAN BAKUCHI)

Very simple Japanese dice game. Six dice are rolled and the results kept secret. Players bet on whether the sum on the dice is odd or even. But with a twist! ...

The player starts out with $500 and if they win a bet (turn) then they get to keep their current dollar amount. If they loose the bet, then $100 would be subtracted from their current totals.
After 5 turns (bets) if they have money they get to keep the result.
